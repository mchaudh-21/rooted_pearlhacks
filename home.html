Go Green
on a Budget!



We wanted to create a website that provides
incentives to live more sustainably. The problem that
comes with this is that many products that are
advertised as sustainable alternatives tend to be
more expensive, therefore less accessible for those
who live on a budget. We want to provide
that accessibility by providing cost-efficient and
eco-friendly products everyone can invest in!
